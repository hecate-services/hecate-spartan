# 013 — The scope of the negative, and the frontier that remains

**Status:** scope correction. The signed negative of 011 stands, exactly as narrow as
it was measured. What is retracted is a conversational **over-reach** — "Spartan is
over, the name is dead" — which generalized a subset-of-faculties result into a
program-level verdict. That leap is borrowed rigor: a strategic mood wearing the
referee's authority, the precise move this program exists to punish. The operator
caught it; the adversary conceded it. This note records what was actually killed,
what remains standing, and why the distinction matters.

## ELI5

We proved one *specific* kind of remembering is a bad idea in one *specific* kind of
world — and then, in the heat of it, two machines announced the whole project dead.
The human in the room said "not so fast — you tested one thing against a simple
world, and you didn't touch several others." He was right. "This exact mechanism
fails here" is a fact we measured. "The whole idea is finished" is a guess we dressed
up as a fact. Big difference. Here's the real, careful line between what we know and
what we only suspect.

## What 011 actually kills (mechanism-based, so generalization is licensed)

Not "memory." Precisely this:

> **Any memory whose recall reproduces the old regime's input→output mapping
> *verbatim*** — whether stored as exemplars, replayed weights, or a settled
> attractor — **in a world where recurrence is parameter-jittered and fresh data is
> cheap.**

The oracle probe pinned the *mechanism*: the failure is **staleness of verbatim
recall**, not poor recognition (a 100%-precision matcher lost too). Because the kill
is mechanism-level, generalizing it is legitimate — unlike counting how many rounds
we argued, which proves nothing. And note the honest boundary of the boundary: we
falsified the pre-registered **kernel contract** (engine-agnostic, canonical payload,
the θ-ban), deliberately chosen — not a strawman by negligence — but the kill's scope
*is that contract*, not Gene Sher's architecture.

## What it leaves standing (untested, not disproven)

- **Abstraction-memory:** recall the *shape/family* of the rule and let fast
  adaptation fill in the current parameters (recall the theme, relearn the numbers).
  Staleness bites verbatim recall; it need not bite an abstraction that is *meant* to
  be re-parameterized.
- **Memory as learned dynamics** rather than stored data — closer to what evolvable
  networks (Sher's actual lineage) embody, and never in the dock here.
- **Every faculty that operates on the PRESENT, not the past** — self-audit,
  verification, deliberation. Staleness is a property of stored history; a faculty
  that reads the current input has nothing to go stale.

## The retraction, and the division of labour it teaches

Two things were conflated and must not be:
1. A **signed mechanism-negative** (011) — measured, narrow, licensed to generalize
   along its mechanism.
2. A **program verdict** ("Spartan is over") — unmeasured, broad, and therefore
   exactly the unfalsifiable mood we retired AGI-as-telos for being.

The adversary produced (1) impeccably and then over-reached into (2); a human refused
the over-reach. That is not the loop failing — it is the loop **working as designed**:
the adversarial process tests *specific claims*; the *scope of conclusions* is set by
human judgement. Round-count is not evidence. Reputation (Sher's) is not evidence
either — deferring to it would be condescension, not respect; the respect is to test
the *actual* mechanism, which we have not yet done.

## The frontier (what a disciplined program tests next)

- **Self-audit / mindfulness** — the deployed minds' one *built* untested faculty
  (draft+verify), turned off for throughput without ever measuring quality. Escapes
  the staleness wall; live only where verification is easier than generation.
  Pre-registered as Experiment M1 (note 014).
- **Abstraction-memory**, in a *compositional* world (regimes as parameterized
  instances of shared latent families, long horizons) — the single world-extension
  that tests the strongest 011-surviving memory class and the expensive-relearning
  corner at once.
- **Sher's actual mechanism** (plasticity inside evolved dynamics) — the largest
  build, shelved until a compositional world exists to test it *fairly*, so it isn't
  re-run inside the jittered-verbatim world whose kill we already own.

## The one line

We killed one mechanism cleanly and then nearly buried a program on the strength of a
mood. The correction is the finding: *verbatim recall dies under drift; abstraction,
learned dynamics, and present-tense faculties are still open* — and the next thing we
believe about any of them will have a number attached before we believe it.
